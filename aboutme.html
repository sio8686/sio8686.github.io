About Me

6,400 Miles Away From Home...

Håfa Adai ~ My name is Sephora, a Chinese-Filipino girl from the beautiful island of Guåhan, born in the Philippines and proud to call the village of Tamuning-Tumon-Harmon in Guam, home -- a small paradise in the Pacific Ocean. People often ask 'Where is Guam?' and 'What is there to see on Guam?' For those of us who are from Guam, we often miss the beautiful waters and serene sunsets that adorn our island of 100,000 in population. Here, I seek to document the sights, sounds, and tastes of Guåhan and to share our beautiful island paradise.
